HONEY BBQ CHICKEN WINGS

Prepared By Morgan Kihara

INGREDIENTS for 20 wings

  • 1 cup flour(125g)
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper,freshly ground
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 20chicken wings or drumettes
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce(290g)
  • 1/2 cup honey(170g)

PREPARATION

  • Preheat oven to (220c
  • In a bowl,combine flour,chili powder,salt,pepper,paprika and garlic powder
  • Coat the wings in the flour evenly,shaking off any excess
  • Place the floure wings on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet and spread them out in a single layer
  • Bake for 45 minutes,,flipping halfway through,until skin is crispy and golden brown
  • Preheat oven to (250c)
  • In a separate bowl,combine the BBQ sauce an the honey.Stir the cooked wings in the sauce an coat them evenly..
  • Place the coated wings back onto the baking sheet and spreadd them in a single layer.
  • Bake for 8 to 10 minutes,until sauce is bubbly and caremelized.cool,then serve.
  • ENJOY!
